Hot Cheetos with Nacho Cheese

Description

Although it may be hard, please don't eat this as a whole meal!

Your stomach may become really upset with you.

Ingredients

  • bag of Flamin' Hot Cheetos
  • nacho cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up nacho cheese on the stovetop
  2. Create a nice layer of Flamin' Hot Cheetos on a plate
  3. Pour warmed cheese on top
  4. Serve while warm

Caution! This snack can be addicting
